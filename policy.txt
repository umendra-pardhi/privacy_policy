The app has internet access for only login purpose, but it is not sending anything outside the device whatsoever.

Information being collected

Simple SMS Messenger does not collect any personal identifiable information.

The 'READ_SMS' permission is used at reading sms from specific declared phone number (specially sms is received from IoT device) and not other phone numbers.

The 'RECIEVE_SMS' permission is to receive sms and generate Notification when sms is Received.

The app can access the sms only from specific phone number declared in app and not other phone numbers , but it is not being sent anywhere outside of the device.
